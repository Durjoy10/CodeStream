About Us

CodeStream is a platform created by Arthur Akaid Islam, a passionate computer science engineer from Daffodil International University. With a vision to provide a space for programmers to showcase their projects, learn from each other, and build a supportive community, CodeStream was born.

Arthur's journey in the world of programming started at an early age, fueled by curiosity and a desire to create. Over the years, he honed his skills in various programming languages and technologies, eventually specializing in web development and software engineering.

Driven by a mission to make coding accessible to everyone, Arthur founded CodeStream as a hub for developers of all levels to collaborate, share knowledge, and inspire innovation. Whether you're a seasoned programmer or just starting out, CodeStream is here to support you on your coding journey.
